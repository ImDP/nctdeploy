About Us

We're a team of innovators dedicated to helping governments and Central Banks explore a future with Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs). Some of the things we do:

  • Conduct R&D, Design CBDCs and Advise on Implementation
  • Develop Intellectual Property
  • Consultation Services for Governments and Central Banks

While it's important to understand what we do, it's also important to know what we don't do. NCT is not in the business of making, creating, trading, distributing, investing or providing investment advice on crypto currencies, distributed ledger or blockchain. We're here to help Governments and Central Banks implement digital versions oftheir national currencies, "Central Bank Digital Currencies" often abbreviated "CBDC".
